About me

My name is Ashley Gloeckner, and I work as a graphic designer. I spend me free time creating art by making resin castings and sewn projects. I love to share them with others, so please contact me if you would like something made!

I'm passionate about animals, so I donate 10% of all my pet related project earning to my favorite shelter: KC Pet Project.

So far, my projects have consisted of pet ID tags, ring holders, trays, key wristlets, wine stoppers, bandanas, bowties, and more. Please message me through email or Instagram. You can also fill out a contact form here. Thank you so much for your support and taking the time to visit my site!
